## 💫 The Experience

When Khushi enters this space, she'll find:

* **The Blue Rose Portal:** A glowing, interactive core that hums with a sine-wave chime when tapped.
* **Shooting Stars & Fireflies:** A real-time rendered night sky that follows her movement.
* **The 3D Polaroid:** A custom-engineered photo frame with perspective-shift physics—it tilts as she explores the screen.
* **Celestial Petal Rain:** A celebratory burst of roses, stars, and sparkles triggered upon reveal.

---

## 🛠️ The "Magic" (Tech Stack)

I used these tools to build this atmosphere for her:
* **Canvas API:** To render the 160+ unique stars and shooting star trajectories.
* **Web Audio API:** To generate the custom "entry chimes" without needing external files.
* **CSS3 Perspective:** To create the depth and 3D tilt effect on her polaroid.
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** To coordinate the petal physics and the midnight parallax background.
